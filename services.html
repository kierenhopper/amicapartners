Amica helps its clients achieve competitive advantage by the identification, assessment and recruitment of leading talent in the industry. When the strategic use of Executive Search is required we work in unison with our clients to pinpoint their needs and develop their message.

All Search projects follow a similar structure:
  • Definition of needs - we work closely with client to define their business needs and understand their specific circumstance
  • Identification - consultants use their network, colleague's network and systematic research
  • Approach - the consultant who leads the project will lead approaches, meetings and management of candidate throughout the process
  • Assessment - by only working in one sector, we can make the critical judgement on how candidates compare to the best in the market
  • Closure - advise and facilitate during the negotiation process minimising any barriers to closure
  • Ongoing support - Our consultants build a long term relationships with candidates and regularly follow up with regards to fit and performance
It is important to stress that there is no delegation of critical activity in our firm. The consultant that takes the brief, leads the research, conducts all interviews and delivers the clients message without dilution.
