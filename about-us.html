Amica Partners is a specialist executive search organisation founded in 2002 by a mix of experienced search consultants, former consulting professionals and in-house corporate recruitment staff. This diverse collection of experience allows us to understand and actively pursue the top talent across all industry verticals and competency lines in EMEA.
Our work is professional, discrete, intelligent, focused and relevant. We provide a high value service tailored to the client's requirements and our success is gauged by quality of work and long lasting relationships, rather than quantity. Our clients are defined nearly exclusively by the quality of the people they employ.

Our service is as individual as each client's long term goals or candidate's career aspirations. We do not participate in the human capital agenda; we secure critical talent, not commodities. We partner for the long term and our client's long term corporate objectives are always our priority.

We treat client selection as critical as the due diligence of candidates who make up our shortlists.
